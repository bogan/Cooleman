Blackberry Survey

The Cooleman Ridge Parkcare Group undertakes a blackberry survey. These blackberry bushers are surveyed mainly to allow the management of an eradication program.

Blackberry bushes are major weed on the ridge, particulary on the eastern end near Mt Arawang.

The group notifies the Parks and Conservation Service and they organised for them to be sprayed as part of the annual blackberry control program.

The map below details the precicse location of individual blackberry bushes that have been identified on the ridge. You can click on each marker to obtain the precise information about each individual occurence.
