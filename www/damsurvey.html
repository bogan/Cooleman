Dam Survey

The Cooleman Ridge Parkcare Group conducts an ongoing Dam Survey. The main objective of the survey is to idenifiy all plant species that occur or have occured on the ridge. This includes both native and exotic plants.

A survey consists of series of almost 300 photos of the dam between 2003 and 2012. The photos were been taken by Pauline and Gösta Lyngå. Unfortunately, photos taken before January 2003 were destroyed in a bushfire. The photographic survey of the dam is continued by Arminel and Pat Ryan.
