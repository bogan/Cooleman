Location

Cooleman Ridge is is a lowing lying ridge made that located in the south western suburbs of Canberra .. It is bordered by the Canberra suburbs of Chapman, Fisher and Kambah.

The Cooleman Ridge in a general north-west to south-east direction. It is approximately 3.3km long on the north-west to south-west axis, and averages approximately 500m in width in the north-east to south-west direction. The average height above sea level is 600 meteres and the reserver has an area of approximately 187 hectare or nearly 2 square km.

Cooleman Ridge is part of the Cooleman Ridge Nature Reserve as protected by the ACT Reserve System. Cooleman Ridge is part of the Canberra Nature Park reserve system. The reserve itself is part of the Canberra Nature Park.
