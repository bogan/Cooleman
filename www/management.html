Management

Cooleman Ridge is part of the Cooleman Ridge Nature Reserve and is protected by the ACT Reserve System. The reserve itself is part of the Canberra Nature Park.

Rules

Because Cooleman Ridge is part of the Cooleman Ridge Nature Reserve, the following rules apply.

  • Dogs are allowed but must be on a leash
  • Biclycles are allowed but only on the vehicular tracks
  • Horses are allowed but only on the Bicentential Trail
  • Motor vehicles are not allowed
