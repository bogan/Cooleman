Meetings

The Cooleman Ridge Parkcare Group has monthly work meetings. These are held the Third Sunday of Month. The group works on one of our designated group work areas.

The montly meetings achieve the following goals

  • Work intensively on a specific are of the ridge
  • Exchange group news and gossip
  • Motivate
  • Have a cup of tea and cake!
  • Introduce new members to the group

Please see our latest newsletter for the specific details for our next meeting.

There are also smaller weekly work groups that focus on just one specific area of the nature reserve. These include:

  • Kathner Street Group - every Friday morning at the Kathner Street Dam site
  • Arawang Group - every Wednesday morning at the Mt Arawang site

Meetings are the main opportunity where hands on work is performed. The group has various meetings including the following:

Group Meetings

The group meets once a month for a group working party. The group works on one of our formally designated group work areas. The group will perform many various activities (eg. weeding, surveying, photography etc) in accordance with our accepted strategy.

Group Meetings also have many other benefits such as exchanging information and a coup of tea and cake afterwards.

The Group Meeting is normally held on the third Sunday of every month (except when there are public holidays such as Christmas or Easter). Please see our latest newsletter for the specific details for the exact time and place for our next meeting.

Weekly Meetings

There are also smaller weekly work groups that focus on just one specific area of the nature reserve. These include:

  • Kathner Street Group - every Friday morning at the Kathner Street Dam site
  • Arawang Group - every Wednesday morning at the Mt Arawang site

These groups are made up of a smaller subset of the group and focus on a particular area outside of our regular group areas.

Annual General Meeting

The group holds an annual general meeting every October. The primary purpose of the meeting is to elect the committee members for the forthcoming year.

The committee then also performs strategic planning for the next year.
